PB&J Sandwich

Description: A peanut butter and jelly sandwich consists of two pieces of bread covered in peanut butter and jelly

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Spread jelly on one slice of bread
  3. Place both slices of bread together
